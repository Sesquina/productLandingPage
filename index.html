Our Story

Contracts are the backbone of an enterprise. Blockchain technology has had important implications when it comes to contract management.

Here at Ledger we assist governments creating secure databases of land titles to consortiums of leading financial institutions working together, to design new distributed ledger technologies.

Ledger was born to help others going through the maze that is Blockchain technology.

Technology

Ledger is the next generation of contract management technology. The digitization of paper-based contracts onto the blockchain holds the potential to revolutionize the way contracts are prepared, transacted, amended, stored and complied with.

Blockchain for Contracts holds the potential to create value for organizations by having a single repository for contracts that indisputably houses latest, agreed-upon versions.
